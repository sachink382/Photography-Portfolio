Sachin Kumar — photographer

I like images that feel observational and honest — built from real light, real people and the small in–between moments on the way from one place to another.

I’m Sachin, a photographer and traveller from India. My work lives somewhere between travel, streets and intimate portraits — sometimes even the quieter side of weddings.

A lot of my photographs come from solo trips, long walks and unplanned detours: bus stands, chai stops, back alleys, early-morning markets and those half–awake hours before the day properly begins.

When I’m shooting for clients, I try to keep the same energy: calm, observant and respectful of the space.

Selected focus
  • Travel journeys & road stories
  • Streets, markets & everyday life
  • Intimate weddings & smaller gatherings
  • Portraits & people–led editorial work
